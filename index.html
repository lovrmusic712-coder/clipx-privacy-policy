Privacy Policy for ClipX

Last Updated: November 2025

ClipX is a free video downloader tool that allows users to download and save public videos from supported social media platforms such as TikTok, Instagram, and X (Twitter).

1. Information We Collect

ClipX does not require user registration and does not collect personally identifiable information. However, we use Google AdMob SDK to serve ads. AdMob may collect device identifiers, ad interaction data, and other analytics for personalized or non-personalized ads.

2. How We Use Information

Information collected by AdMob is used only to improve ad relevance and measure ad performance.

3. Data Sharing

ClipX does not share or sell any user data. Only AdMob, as a third-party service, processes limited data in compliance with Google’s privacy policies.

4. Data Security

We do not store user data. All downloads occur directly between the user’s device and the target platform’s public servers.

5. Children’s Privacy

ClipX is not directed at children under 13 years of age.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, you can contact us at swiftharbor@gmail.com.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy at any time. Updates will be posted on this page.

Thank you for using ClipX!
